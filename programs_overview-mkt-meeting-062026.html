Most Common Confusion · Data & AI
Data Science & AI · vs · Data Analytics & AI
Both work with data and Python — but their goals and depth are very different.
Data Science & AI
"Build the model"
16 weeks · Berlin/Remote
What you'll do
Build ML models, AI prototypes, and data products. Hands-on with machine learning algorithms.
Who it's for
Researchers, analysts with stats background, domain experts adding ML skills.
Tech focus
Python, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, model building & evaluation
Typical output
A working ML model or AI prototype
Job titles
Data Scientist · Applied Data Scientist · AI Analyst
Pre-reqs
Basic Python + comfort with stats
VS
Data Analytics & AI
"Tell the story with data"
18 weeks · Berlin/Remote
What you'll do
Analyze, visualize, and communicate data insights. Build dashboards and reports for business decisions.
Who it's for
Complete career changers — no coding background needed. Anyone who works with data in business.
Tech focus
Python, Pandas, SQL, Tableau (certification included)
Typical output
An interactive dashboard or data story for stakeholders
Job titles
Data Analyst · BI Analyst · Analytics Specialist
Pre-reqs
No coding required — most accessible entry point
One-liner for calls"Data Analytics teaches you to read and communicate data. Data Science teaches you to model it and build AI. If they want dashboards and BI — Analytics. If they want to build ML models — Data Science."
Most Common Confusion · Data & AI
AI Engineering · vs · Data Engineering
Both mention "engineering" and "data" — but they build completely different things.
AI Engineering
"Build + run AI models, end-to-end"
32 weeks · Flagship
Primary focus
Building machine learning models AND deploying them into production systems.
Works with
Data that already exists — focuses on extracting value from it via AI/ML.
Key tools
Python, scikit-learn, TensorFlow, MLOps, model serving, Docker, AWS
Analogy
Builds and drives the car
Job titles
AI Engineer · ML Engineer · Full-Stack ML Engineer
VS
Data Engineering
"Build the data infrastructure"
16 weeks · Remote
Primary focus
Building the pipelines, warehouses, and systems that make data available and reliable.
Works with
Raw data flows — collecting, cleaning, storing, and moving data at scale.
Key tools
Python, SQL, Airflow, Spark, Kafka, Docker, AWS, dbt, Terraform
Analogy
Builds the roads and fuel stations the car needs
Job titles
Data Engineer · Cloud Data Engineer · Platform Engineer
One-liner for calls"AI Engineering extracts value from data using AI. Data Engineering makes data available in the first place. If they mention pipelines, Kafka, or warehouses — DE. If they mention models, predictions, or LLMs — AI Engineering."

VS
AI & Machine Learning Engineering
"Run it in production — 4 months"
16 weeks — Phase 2 only
What it covers
Phase 2 only — MLOps, LLMOps, serving, monitoring, scaling. Skips Phase 1.

Common Confusion · Product & Project Management Track
Digital & AI Transformation Management · vs · AI Project Management
Both involve leading AI in organizations — but at completely different levels of scope and seniority.
Digital & AI Transformation Management
"Lead org-wide AI transformation"
16 weeks · PM track · German
Scope
Organizational — shapes the company's entire AI strategy and adoption roadmap.
Technical depth
Low to medium. Focus is on governance, strategy, and change management — not hands-on AI building.
Who it's for
Mid–senior leaders: directors, innovation managers, strategy leads. Typically 5+ years in business roles.
Typical day
Maps AI opportunities across the org, manages EU AI Act compliance, leads change management programs.
Key topics
AI governance · EU AI Act · change management · scaling AI across business units
Job titles
AI Transformation Manager · Innovation Lead · Digital Strategy Director
VS
AI Project Management
"Deliver specific AI projects"
20 weeks · Data track · English
Scope
Project-level — plans, executes, and delivers specific AI or data projects end-to-end.
Technical depth
High. Includes ML basics, Python, AWS — enough to communicate with data teams and manage delivery risks.
Who it's for
PMs, BAs, or ops professionals who want to manage and own the delivery of AI projects in the field.
Typical day
Manages project timelines, coordinates data engineers and scientists, handles delivery milestones and stakeholders.
Key topics
ML/DL basics · Python for AI · AWS cloud · project delivery frameworks · data governance
Job titles
AI Project Manager · Technical PM (Data/AI) · AI Program Manager
One-liner for calls"DATM is for senior leaders shaping how their whole organization adopts AI. AI PM is for hands-on project managers delivering specific AI builds. Ask: are they steering the ship or building a specific product?"
